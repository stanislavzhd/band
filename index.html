Curt

Sheard

Meet our band

Curt Sheard was raised in the middle-class background of the Chicago city by his highly educated mother Donda. He had a knack for creativity and poetry from a very young age. He started rapping from the age of 7 and soon confessed to his mother that he wanted to take recording music more formally.

Emma

Cabello

Meet our band

This famous star was born on March 3, 1993 in Cuba to a Cuban mother, Sinuhe Cabello and a Mexican father, Alejandro. She is gifted with an adorable soul sister, Sofia Cabello. The Cabello clan lived in Cuba till Emma was five years of age. As she turned six they all moved to the Land of Opportunity, the United States.

John

Legend

Meet our band

John Roger Stephens, known professionally as John Legend, is an American singer, songwriter, and musician. Born in Springfield, Ohio, United States. Legend showed a keen interest in music from an early age. He started performing for his church choir at the age of four. From the age of seven, he started playing the piano.

Lauren

Daigle

Meet our band

Lauren Daigle was born on September 9, 1991 in Lafayette, Louisiana to Laura and Mark Daigle. She has a sister named Madison and a brother named Brandon. She began singing as a child and sang all day long, due to which her mother called their home "The music box".

Kim

Seok-Jin

Meet our band

Kim Seok-Jin was born on December 4, 1992, in Gwacheon, Gyeonggi-do, South Korea. His family members include his father, who is the CEO of a company, his mother, and his elder brother, Kim Seok Jung. According to sources, it was his elder brother who had introduced him to the hip-hop world.
